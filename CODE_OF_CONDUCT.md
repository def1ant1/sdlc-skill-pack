# Code of Conduct

This project follows a respectful, inclusive, and professional collaboration standard.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code, documentation, and outcomes.
- Assume good intent.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Abusive or insulting language.

## Reporting

Report concerns to maintainers through repository issue templates or designated maintainer contacts.
