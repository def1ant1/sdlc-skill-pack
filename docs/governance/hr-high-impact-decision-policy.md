# HR High-Impact Decision Policy

## Purpose
Constrain automation for HR decisions that materially affect people.

## Policy
- Hiring, compensation, performance actions, disciplinary actions, and termination-related recommendations are support-only.
- Human-sensitive bias checks are required for high-impact HR outputs.
- Final HR decisions require qualified human reviewers and documented rationale.
- AI-generated HR outputs must not be the sole basis for adverse action.

## Required Controls
- Mandatory review gates for high-impact HR categories.
- Bias and fairness review checklist.
- Candidate/employee data handling aligned with privacy and retention policies.


## Performance Insight Decision Gate (MB-P2-006)
- Performance insights are advisory only and **must not** auto-trigger employment actions.
- Before any employment-impact use, a named human reviewer must confirm:
  - protected-attribute exclusion checks passed;
  - fairness safeguards passed or documented exceptions were approved;
  - rationale and evidence are stored in the audit trail.
- If checks fail or are incomplete, the workflow must block downstream employment-impact actions.
