# Learning & Development Fairness and Employment-Impact Governance Policy

## Purpose
Define enforceable safeguards for HR/L&D analytics, recommendations, and performance insights.

## Mandatory Controls
1. **No protected-attribute scoring**
   - Protected/sensitive attributes and known proxies are prohibited in scoring, ranking, and recommendation features.
2. **Fairness validation required**
   - Subgroup disparity and adverse-impact checks are required pre-release for L&D and performance insight outputs.
3. **Human decision authority**
   - Any output that may influence hiring, promotion, compensation, discipline, termination, or role assignment must be reviewed and approved by an authorized human reviewer.
4. **Auditability**
   - Fairness check artifacts, feature inclusion/exclusion logs, and reviewer sign-off must be retained in the audit trail.

## Protected Attributes (minimum baseline)
- age, race, ethnicity, color, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, religion, national origin/citizenship, pregnancy/parental status, veteran status.

## L&D Outcome and Certification Tracking Requirements
- Programs must record baseline and post-learning outcomes.
- Certification workflows must track assignment, completion, expiration, and renewal states.
- Overdue or exception states must route to a human governance queue.
