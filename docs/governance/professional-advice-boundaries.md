# Professional Advice Boundaries Policy

## Purpose
Define boundaries for AI-generated support in regulated professional domains.

## Policy
- Outputs are support-only and must not be treated as final legal, tax, payroll, accounting, investment, or employment determinations.
- Any high-impact recommendation in regulated domains requires qualified human review before execution.
- AI outputs must include uncertainty notes, assumptions, and required review roles when applicable.
- The system must block autonomous final determinations for regulated outcomes.

## Required Controls
- Human-in-the-loop approval for final determinations.
- Audit trail including reviewer identity, decision timestamp, and rationale.
- Escalation path to domain experts for ambiguous or high-risk scenarios.
