## Section 3 — Security and Compliance Requirements

- [ ] SOC 2 Type II certification (required for [data type])
- [ ] GDPR compliance (required for EU data processing)
- [ ] Response to attached security questionnaire
- [ ] Data residency: [specify if applicable]
- [ ] Incident notification within 24 hours of discovery

---

## Section 4 — Vendor Information Required

Vendors must provide:

1. **Company overview**: Legal name, founding year, employee count, revenue range
2. **References**: 3 customers of similar size and use case (contact information required)
3. **Security**: Current SOC 2 report or completed security questionnaire
4. **Financials**: Evidence of financial stability (D&B report, audited statements, or investor references)
5. **Team**: Key personnel proposed for this engagement

---

## Section 5 — Pricing Requirements

Provide pricing for:

- [ ] Base implementation / setup fee
- [ ] Monthly / annual subscription (by tier if applicable)
- [ ] Per-unit pricing (if usage-based)
- [ ] Professional services rate card
- [ ] Total 3-year cost of ownership estimate

Include any assumptions in pricing. Pricing must be valid for 60 days from submission.

---

## Section 6 — Evaluation Criteria

Proposals will be scored on the following weighted criteria:

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| Capability fit to requirements | 30% |
| Total cost of ownership (3-year) | 25% |
| Security and compliance posture | 20% |
| Support quality and SLAs | 15% |
| Strategic alignment and roadmap | 10% |

**Minimum score to advance**: 3.0/5.0 on Capability Fit (any score below is disqualifying).

---

## Section 7 — Proposal Format

Submit your proposal as a single PDF or structured document:

1. Executive Summary (max 1 page)
2. Technical Response (address each requirement in Section 2)
3. Security Response (address Section 3)
4. Vendor Information (Section 4)
5. Pricing (Section 5)
6. References
7. Appendices (supporting documents, certifications)

---

- Apotheon.ai reserves the right to reject any or all proposals
- Vendors may not contact team members directly during the RFP period
- Questions must be submitted by [date] to [email]
