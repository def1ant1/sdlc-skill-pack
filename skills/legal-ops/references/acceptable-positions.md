# Acceptable Positions

## Overview

This document defines the company's negotiating positions for each key contract term.
Three positions are defined: Preferred, Acceptable, and Hard Limit (never accept).

Legal counsel must be consulted before accepting any position at or beyond the Hard Limit.

---

## Liability

| Term | Preferred | Acceptable | Hard Limit (Never Accept) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Liability cap | 12 months of fees paid | 6–12 months | < 6 months; uncapped Company liability |
| Consequential damages | Mutual exclusion | One-sided (Company excluded) | Company bears uncapped consequential damages |
| Indemnification | Mutual | Company indemnified by vendor for IP claims | One-sided indemnification against Company |

---

## Intellectual Property

| Term | Preferred | Acceptable | Hard Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Work product ownership | Company owns all work product | Joint ownership with license to Company | Vendor retains ownership of custom work |
| Background IP | Each party retains its own | License grant to Company for background IP | Company must assign background IP to vendor |
| AI-generated output | Company owns | Shared commercial license | Vendor owns output from Company's prompts |
| Open source | Company approved OSS list | Notify and get approval | Copyleft licenses in proprietary code |

---

## Data

| Term | Preferred | Acceptable | Hard Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Data processing | DPA per our template | Vendor's DPA (reviewed by legal) | No DPA for PII processing |
| Data use by vendor | Processing only; no training | Aggregated / anonymized for product improvement | Training on Company's customer data |
| Sub-processors | Pre-approved list only | Notify 30 days before adding | No sub-processor restrictions |
| Data retention | Delete within 30 days of contract end | Delete within 90 days | Retain Company data indefinitely |
| International transfer | EU SCCs in place | Adequacy decision | No mechanism for EU→non-adequate transfer |

---

## Commercial Terms

| Term | Preferred | Acceptable | Hard Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Payment terms | Net-45 | Net-30 | Payment in advance (unless < $1K or established vendor) |
| Auto-renewal notice | 30 days | 60 days | > 90 days |
| Price increase | No increase; or CPI-capped | < 5% annual increase with 90-day notice | Uncapped price increase mid-term |
| Currency | USD | Major currencies with FX clause | Obscure currency or unfavorable FX mechanism |

---

## Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

| Term | Preferred | Acceptable | Hard Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Governing law | Company's home state | Neutral state (Delaware, New York) | Vendor's home country (non-US) without mutual consent |
| Dispute resolution | Negotiation → Mediation → Arbitration | Arbitration (AAA or JAMS) | Foreign arbitration or courts without mutual consent |
| Venue | Company home city | Neutral venue | Vendor's home city (impractical for Company) |
| Class action waiver | Included | — | Prohibited (if Company is exposed to class claims) |

---

## Termination

| Term | Preferred | Acceptable | Hard Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Termination for convenience | 30-day notice by Company | 60-day notice | > 90-day notice or not permitted |
| Termination for cause | Immediate on material breach; 10-day cure | 30-day cure period | No right to terminate for cause |
| Effect of termination | Vendor deletes all data within 30 days | 90 days with certification | Vendor retains data after termination |

---

## Confidentiality

| Term | Preferred | Acceptable | Hard Limit |
|---|---|---|---|
| Confidentiality period | 3 years post-disclosure | 2 years | < 1 year for trade secrets |
| Residuals clause | Not included | Included with no exceptions for source code | Broad residuals including source code |
