# Content Brief Template

## Content Brief

```
CONTENT BRIEF
=============
Title:          <working title>
Brief ID:       CB-YYYYMMDD-NNN
Type:           blog | guide | whitepaper | case-study | video-script | newsletter | landing-page
Author:         <assigned writer>
Status:         Draft | In Review | Approved | Published
Due Date:       YYYY-MM-DD
Publish Date:   YYYY-MM-DD

OBJECTIVE
  Goal:         <what this content achieves — traffic | leads | retention | brand>
  Target KPI:   <specific metric and target>
  Audience:     <primary persona — e.g., "Senior DevOps engineers at 50–500-person SaaS companies">
  Search intent: Informational | Navigational | Commercial | Transactional

SEO TARGETING
  Primary keyword:     <target keyword>
  Secondary keywords:  [list 3–5]
  Target ranking:      Top 10 for "<keyword>"
  Current position:    <N or "not ranking">
  Competitor URLs:     [top 3 ranking URLs to analyze and outperform]

CONTENT OUTLINE
  Suggested word count: N
  H1: <title>
  H2: <section 1>
    - Key point 1
    - Key point 2
  H2: <section 2>
    - Key point
  H2: <section 3>
  [CTA]: <call to action — what we want the reader to do>

KEY MESSAGES
  1. [Core message the reader must take away]
  2. [Second key message]
  3. [Third key message]

EVIDENCE AND SOURCES
  - [Statistic or claim + source]
  - [Research paper or report to cite]
  - [Internal data or case study to reference]

TONE AND STYLE
  Tone: Technical | Accessible | Authoritative | Conversational
  POV: First person (we) | Second person (you) | Third person
  Reading level: Expert | Practitioner | General
  Avoid: [any terms or framing to avoid]

INTERNAL LINKS
  Link from this piece to:
  - [URL + anchor text]
  Link to this piece from:
  - [URL + anchor text]

DISTRIBUTION
  Channels: Blog | Newsletter | LinkedIn | Twitter/X | Syndication
  Promotion plan: <brief notes on amplification>
```

---

## Quality Checklist (Pre-Publication)

- [ ] Primary keyword appears in H1, first paragraph, and at least 2 H2s
- [ ] Word count meets target (±10%)
- [ ] All claims are cited or verifiable
- [ ] Internal links added (minimum 3 to existing content)
- [ ] Meta title: ≤ 60 characters, includes primary keyword
- [ ] Meta description: 150–160 characters, compelling, includes keyword
- [ ] Images have descriptive alt text
- [ ] CTA is clear and links to correct destination
- [ ] Content reviewed by subject matter expert
- [ ] No PII or confidential information
- [ ] Legal/compliance review completed (if making product claims)

## Content Types and Standards

| Type | Word Count | Promotion | Lifespan |
|---|---|---|---|
| Blog post | 800–1,500 | Newsletter + social | 6–12 months |
| Long-form guide | 2,000–5,000 | All channels | 12–24 months (evergreen) |
| Case study | 600–1,000 | Sales + newsletter | Until customer churns |
| Whitepaper | 3,000–8,000 | Gated download | 12–18 months |
| Newsletter issue | 400–800 | Email only | 1 week |
| Landing page | 400–800 | Paid + SEO | Until offer changes |
