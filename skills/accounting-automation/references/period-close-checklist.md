# Period Close Checklist

## Monthly Close (Business Day 3–5)

### Day 1–2: Data Collection

- [ ] All bank statements received and available
- [ ] All credit card statements received
- [ ] All vendor invoices for the period processed and posted
- [ ] Payroll entries confirmed with HR
- [ ] All expense reports approved and posted
- [ ] Revenue recognized per subscription billing system

### Day 2–3: Reconciliation

- [ ] Bank accounts reconciled (all discrepancies investigated)
- [ ] Credit card accounts reconciled
- [ ] AR aging review — flag accounts > 60 days overdue
- [ ] AP aging review — confirm no past-due payables
- [ ] Deferred revenue schedule updated
- [ ] Prepaid expense amortization posted

### Day 3–4: Adjustments and Review

- [ ] Depreciation and amortization entries posted
- [ ] Accruals posted (unbilled vendor expenses, payroll accrual)
- [ ] AI compute cost allocation posted (from runtime-economics)
- [ ] All anomaly flags from accounting-automation reviewed and resolved
- [ ] Trial balance reviewed for unusual variances (> 10% MoM)

### Day 4–5: Financial Statements

- [ ] P&L statement produced and reviewed
- [ ] Balance sheet produced and reviewed
- [ ] Cash flow statement produced and reviewed
- [ ] Comparison to budget variance table produced
- [ ] Management commentary drafted (key variances explained)

### Close Approval (CFO Sign-Off Required)

- [ ] All reconciliations signed off by Finance Manager
- [ ] Trial balance approved
- [ ] Financial statements reviewed and approved by CFO
- [ ] Period locked in accounting system

---

## Quarterly Close (Additional Items)

- [ ] All monthly items completed
- [ ] Quarter-end accruals reviewed (bonuses, commissions, warranties)
- [ ] Revenue recognition review (ASC 606 compliance)
- [ ] Equity compensation expense reviewed
- [ ] Tax provision calculation (if applicable)
- [ ] Board-level financial package prepared
- [ ] Quarterly reforecast updated

---

## Annual Close (Additional Items)

- [ ] All quarterly items completed
- [ ] Year-end audit package prepared
- [ ] Fixed asset register reviewed and reconciled
- [ ] Goodwill and intangibles impairment assessment
- [ ] Related party transactions reviewed
- [ ] Commitment and contingencies footnote prepared
- [ ] Management representation letter prepared
- [ ] External auditor engagement completed
- [ ] Annual financial statements finalized

---

## Approval Authority

| Close Type | Approver |
|---|---|
| Monthly close | CFO (Level-3) |
| Quarterly close | CFO + CEO awareness |
| Annual close | CFO + Board audit committee |
